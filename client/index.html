The Game

Introducing FomoSumo, a one-of-its-kind web-based motion-controlled multiplayer game suite. Okay, but what does this mean?

WEB-BASED: Don't bother downloading anything onto your device. We don't want to hog up any of that precious space. Instead, just simply key in www.fomosumo.com on your mobile browsers and start playing.

MOTION-CONTROLLED: We turned your mobile device into a motion controller. Remember the Nintendo Wii or Switch? Why spend money on those, when the only mobile controller you need is right there in your hands already?

MULTIPLAYER: No more FOMO. Instead of staring at your respective screens, you and your friends can enjoy the ensuring ridiculousness that comes with playing in the same space.

GAME SUITE: That's right, not just one, lonely game. With FomoSumo, the possibilities are endless. Developers can access our library and build their own games.

But honestly, what does all of these really mean? Put simply, ENDLESS FUN.
